About



B.L.A.C.C. Corporation, with the alias B.L.A.C.C., was established in the State of California on June 16, 2020 for the purpose of creating inventions to incorporate under B.L.A.C.C. Corporation as Intellectual Property. The fields that B.L.A.C.C. focus on Biology, Language, Architecture, Computer Science and Change (Humanitarian Work). Stevie W. Carpenter academic background stems from my A.A. of Liberal Arts: Mathematics and Science, A.A. of Liberal Arts: Social and Behavioral Science, A.A. of Liberal Arts: Arts and Humanities, B.S. of Neurobiology w/ minor in Statistics.


New Nexxus was the first invention incorporated under B.L.A.C.C. in 2020, which is a networking system made for the Student Government of The United States. This idea evolved from my involvement in the Student Government as Senator of Activities and Planning, Executive of Outreach and Vice President of Los Angeles City College.
